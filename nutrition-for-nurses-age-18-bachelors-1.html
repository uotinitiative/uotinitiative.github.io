Integrate evidence-based nutrition assessment into holistic nursing care plans.

About This Textbook

The text emphasizes holistic assessment, interdisciplinary collaboration, and culturally aware care to help pre-licensure nurses incorporate nutrition into every patient encounter. Systems-based chapters guide learners from fundamentals to specialized considerations for complex conditions.

Teaching & Learning Tips

Link chapters to nursing process documentation exercises so students practice charting nutrition-focused interventions. Collaborative case reviews with dietetic resources reinforce interprofessional communication skills.
