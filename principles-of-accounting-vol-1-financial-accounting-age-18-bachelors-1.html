About This Textbook

Volume 1 emphasizes financial accounting foundations for both accounting and non-accounting majors. Realistic examples, guided practice, and concept checks reinforce why transactions are recorded, how statements connect, and how stakeholders interpret reported results.

Teaching & Learning Tips

Supplement chapter problems with short spreadsheet exercises so learners see the flow of transactions into financial statements. Encourage reflections that connect accounting ethics and internal controls to current corporate case studies.
