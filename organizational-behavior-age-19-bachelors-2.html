About This Textbook

Chapters blend psychology, sociology, and management perspectives to help students interpret behavior in the workplace. Cases and activities emphasize inclusivity, motivation, conflict resolution, and change management.

Teaching & Learning Tips

Facilitate structured team reflections after group projects to connect OB theory with lived classroom dynamics. Use data from engagement surveys or organizational caselets to practice diagnosing root causes and proposing interventions.
