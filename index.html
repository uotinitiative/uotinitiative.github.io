Universal Open Textbook Initiative

The Universal Open Textbook Initiative is building the world’s largest repository of high-quality, multilingual open textbooks. It was founded by Vatsal, a philosopher and entrepreneur.

Our platform currently offers 79 textbooks in 5 languages, covering subjects from Kindergarten through Master’s. All materials are free to download, share, and adapt.
