Universal Open Textbook Initiative

The Universal Open Textbook Initiative is building the world’s largest repository of high-quality, multilingual open textbooks. It was founded by Vatsal, a philosopher and entrepreneur.

Whether you’re a parent homeschooling in Afghanistan, a science teacher in rural Kenya, or a humanitarian tutoring refugees in Venezuela, UOTI makes world-class educational resources accessible to all, in the languages learners know best.

Our platform currently offers 52 textbooks across 5 languages, with new titles added every month. All materials are free to download, share, and adapt.
