Universal Open Textbook Initiative

The Universal Open Textbook Initiative was founded by the philosopher Vatsal with the goal of providing free, high-quality textbooks for learners of all ages, subjects, and major languages.

You are free to download, share, and modify these textbooks for schools, educational ventures, homeschooling, personal learning, or any other purpose.
