Universal Open Textbook Initiative

The Universal Open Textbook Initiative was founded by the philosopher Vatsal with the goal of providing free, uniform, high quality textbooks to learners of all ages, all subjects, and all major languages. These textbooks can be used for schools, educational ventures, homeschooling, or any other end.
