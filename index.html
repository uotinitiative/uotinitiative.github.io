Universal Open Textbook Initiative

The Universal Open Textbook Initiative, founded by the philosopher Vatsal, aims to provide free, high-quality textbooks for all learners.

You are free to download, share, and adapt these textbooks for use in schools and colleges, educational ventures, homeschooling, personal learning, or any other purpose. Learn more in the FAQ.

Get started by selecting a language, learner age, and subject from the options below. For more information on the options, consult the Master Curriculum Document.
