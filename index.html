Universal Open Textbook Initiative

The Universal Open Textbook Initiative is building the world’s largest repository of high-quality, multilingual open textbooks. Founded by the philosopher Vatsal, the initiative aims to improve the quality and accessibility of educational resources worldwide.

You can download, share, and adapt these textbooks for use in schools and colleges, homeschooling, learning ventures, self-education, or any other purpose. Learn more in the FAQ.

Get started by selecting a language, learner age, and subject from the options below. Or use the Search page to explore the scope and content of each textbook available on our platform.
