About This Textbook

This integrated algebra and trigonometry resource supports varied pacing and course structures. It develops function fluency, explores trigonometric identities and applications, and introduces analytic geometry—providing a strong launching pad toward precalculus and calculus.

Teaching & Learning Tips

Use graphing technology and unit circle visualizations to highlight connections among algebraic and trigonometric representations. Encourage students to verify identities and solve equations using multiple strategies.
