About This Textbook

Designed for entry-level courses, this text surveys economic systems, management, marketing, operations, and finance while spotlighting current trends and diverse business stories. Students gain the vocabulary, frameworks, and cross-disciplinary awareness needed to evaluate how organizations create value.

Teaching & Learning Tips

Invite learners to track a business news story across the semester, connecting chapter topics to real decisions. Small-group simulations that require cross-functional choices help highlight how marketing, finance, operations, and HR intersect.
