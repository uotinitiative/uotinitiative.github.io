About This Textbook

The text meets the scope and sequence of introductory astronomy courses. Learners explore the tools of astronomical observation, the structure and evolution of stars, and the large-scale structure of the universe. Historical perspectives and modern discoveries help connect theory to ongoing research.

Teaching & Learning Tips

Combine readings with planetarium software or observation nights to cement understanding of celestial mechanics. Encourage students to analyze current space mission data and citizen-science projects to connect coursework with real discoveries.
