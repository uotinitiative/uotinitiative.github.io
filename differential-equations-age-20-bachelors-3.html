About This Textbook

Students learn modeling, solution techniques for first and higher-order ODEs, Laplace transforms, systems of equations, and boundary value problems. Applications span engineering, physics, and life sciences.

  • PDF: Ideal for lecture notes, problem sets, and self-paced review.

Use computational tools to visualize solutions and model real-world systems. Encourage interdisciplinary projects where students apply differential equations to their major fields.
