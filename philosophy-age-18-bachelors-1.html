Philosophy (Age 18, US Bachelor’s 1)

Introduce students to the major questions of philosophy—logic, knowledge, reality, ethics, and political thought—with a global perspective.

About This Textbook

The text presents philosophical inquiry through historical and contemporary lenses, highlighting contributions from many cultures. Each chapter integrates discussion prompts and activities that encourage students to examine their own viewpoints.

  • PDF: Suitable for lecture prep, student annotation, and open educational remixing.

Teaching & Learning Tips

Encourage Socratic dialogue, reflective journaling, and cross-cultural case studies to help learners connect abstract concepts to lived experience.
