About This Textbook

Lessons blend reading, experimentation, and data representation to help students reason about the properties of matter, model Earth’s water and climate systems, and analyze energy flow in ecosystems.

Teaching & Learning Tips

Have students design simple investigations comparing mixtures and solutions to practice controlling variables. During Earth science units, use globe-and-flashlight demonstrations to visualize climate zones and seasonal sunlight changes.
