About This Textbook

The first volume of the Advanced Calculus series covers the real number system, sequences and series, continuity, differentiation, integration, and introductory metric space theory, preparing students for graduate-level analysis.

Teaching & Learning Tips

Encourage frequent proof-writing practice and explore applications to topology and functional analysis. Collaborative problem sessions can deepen understanding of abstract concepts.
