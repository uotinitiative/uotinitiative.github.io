About This Textbook

Designed for science majors who are not yet using calculus, the text emphasizes conceptual understanding, worked examples, and practice problems that connect physics to engineering, health sciences, and technology careers.

  • PDF: Ideal for lecture preparation, lab coordination, and offline study.
