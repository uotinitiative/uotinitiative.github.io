About This Textbook

This resource adapts core statistical concepts for business, economics, and related majors. Worked examples, business scenarios, and practice sets reinforce how descriptive and inferential techniques guide everyday commercial decisions.

Teaching & Learning Tips

Have students build dashboards that visualize sample data sets as they progress through descriptive and inferential topics. Case studies drawn from marketing analytics, operations, or finance make abstract formulas more memorable.
