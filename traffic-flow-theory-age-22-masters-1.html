About This Textbook

The book presents the fundamental relationships of traffic variables, shock wave theory, car-following, macroscopic and microscopic models, and applications to node modeling and traffic management.

Table of Contents

  • Traffic Variables and Cumulative Curves
  • Relationships among Traffic Variables
  • Shock Wave Theory and Moving Bottlenecks
  • Traffic States, Car-following, and Lane Change Models
  • Macroscopic Dynamic Traffic Flow Models
  • Node Models and Macroscopic Fundamental Diagrams
  • Method of Characteristics and Headway Models
  • Traffic State Dynamics in Multiple Representations

Teaching & Learning Tips

Encourage students to analyze real traffic datasets and run simulations to observe shock waves, congestion, and control strategies.
