Electromagnetics: Volume 1 (Age 20, US Bachelor’s 3)

About This Textbook

This textbook is designed for electrical engineering students in the third year of a bachelor of science degree program. It is intended as a primary textbook for a one-semester first course in undergraduate engineering electromagnetics.
