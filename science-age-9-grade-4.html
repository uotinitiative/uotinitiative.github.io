About This Textbook

Fourth graders deepen their understanding of energy transfer, light, and electric circuits while studying Earth’s geology and human body systems. Structured lessons combine readings, diagrams, investigations, and reflection prompts.

Teaching & Learning Tips

Integrate cause-and-effect graphic organizers to connect energy concepts with real-world examples. During Earth science units, have students build models or timelines showing erosion and deposition processes to cement understanding.
