Science (Age 5, US Kindergarten)

About This Textbook

This textbook is designed to meet the full scope-and-sequence expectations of a kindergarten science course that nurtures early scientific thinking through simple, observable phenomena. It develops foundational ideas about weather and seasons and about forces and motion, preparing students for deeper study of Earth and physical science in later grades.
