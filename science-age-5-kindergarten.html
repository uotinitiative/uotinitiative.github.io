About This Textbook

This kindergarten science reader encourages observation, conversation, and playful experimentation. Students explore how weather changes around them and how gravity affects the things they drop, slide, and roll.

Teaching & Learning Tips

Use a simple weather station chart so students can record observations over several days, then compare patterns. For gravity, let learners test how different classroom objects fall or roll, and encourage them to explain what they notice.
