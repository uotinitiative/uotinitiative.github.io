About This Textbook

Volume 2 transitions students to managerial accounting applications. Learners analyze cost behavior, construct budgets, interpret variances, and evaluate strategic investments through practice and discussion scenarios tailored to decision makers.

Teaching & Learning Tips

Integrate spreadsheet-based budgeting labs to highlight the impact of assumptions on projections. Management memos or presentation assignments help students explain quantitative analyses to non-accounting stakeholders.
