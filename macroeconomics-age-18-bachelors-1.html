Macroeconomics (Age 18, US Bachelor’s 1)

About This Textbook

This textbook covers the scope and sequence of most one semester introductory macroeconomics courses. The text uses conversational language and ample illustrations to explore economic theories, and provides a wide array of examples using both fictional and real-world applications.
