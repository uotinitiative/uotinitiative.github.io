About This Textbook

Inquiry prompts and hands-on activities lead students to observe shadows, track the Moon, and investigate how living things use their features. Connections to literacy and math help reinforce vocabulary and data skills.

Teaching & Learning Tips

Start each unit with a “notice and wonder” chart to connect new concepts to prior experiences. Encourage students to draw and label diagrams of shadows, instruments, or body systems to strengthen observation skills.
