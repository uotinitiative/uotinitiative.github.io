About This Textbook

Students learn to analyze markets, segment audiences, and deploy the marketing mix through industry examples and data-driven exercises. The narrative highlights digital transformation, sustainability, and inclusive marketing practices.

Teaching & Learning Tips

Have students audit a brand’s omnichannel presence and present SWOT findings tied to specific chapters. Quick analytics labs with freely available datasets illustrate how segmentation and targeting decisions are made.
