Mathematics for Liberal Arts Majors (Age 18, US Bachelor’s 1)

About This Textbook

This textbook is designed to meet the requirements for a liberal arts mathematics course. The textbook covers a range of topics that are typically found in a liberal arts course as well as some topics to connect mathematics to the world around us.
