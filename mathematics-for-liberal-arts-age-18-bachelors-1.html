About This Textbook

This liberal arts mathematics text blends number sense, financial literacy, probability, statistics, and geometry with accessible explanations. Each chapter includes applications that show how mathematical thinking supports civic decision making and everyday life.

Teaching & Learning Tips

Use interdisciplinary projects—such as analyzing election data or budgeting scenarios—to help learners connect mathematics to their interests. Group discussions and reflective writing can reinforce the relevance of quantitative reasoning beyond the classroom.
