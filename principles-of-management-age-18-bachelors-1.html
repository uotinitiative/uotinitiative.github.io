About This Textbook

This traditional management text covers leading, planning, organizing, and controlling while integrating entrepreneurship, strategy, and innovation themes. Learners explore ethical frameworks and global perspectives that underpin effective leadership.

Teaching & Learning Tips

Use mini case studies that require learners to diagnose management issues and recommend interventions. Rotating facilitation roles during discussions helps students practice leadership behaviors in a low-stakes setting.
