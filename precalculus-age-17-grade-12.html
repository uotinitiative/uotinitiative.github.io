About This Textbook

This adaptable text spans more material than a typical one- or two-semester college-level precalculus course, allowing instructors to tailor pacing and depth. It unifies algebraic, graphical, and numerical perspectives while preparing learners for calculus.

Teaching & Learning Tips

Integrate technology for exploring transformations and trigonometric graphs, and offer bilingual vocabulary supports for multilingual classrooms. Encourage students to connect sequences and limits to foundational calculus ideas.
