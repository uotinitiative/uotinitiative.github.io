Quantum Electrical Circuits (Age 23, US Master’s 2)

About This Textbook

This textbook provides a comprehensive overview of circuit quantum electrodynamics or circuit QED. It is intended for theoretically-oriented Master or PhD students in physics and electrical engineering, as well as Master and PhD students who work on experimental superconducting quantum devices and wish to learn more theory.
