Connect foundational biology concepts to everyday life in a one-semester survey tailored for non-science majors.

About This Textbook

This text introduces the scientific method, cell biology, genetics, evolution, and ecology using approachable language and vivid examples. Career highlights and real-world applications help students appreciate how biological principles impact medicine, conservation, and everyday decision making.

Available Formats

  • PDF: Suited for classroom projection, learning management systems, and offline reading.

Table of Contents

  • Cell Division and Genetics
  • Molecular Biology and Biotechnology
  • Evolution and the Diversity of Life
  • Animal Structure and Function
  • Ecology

Teaching & Learning Tips

Use local case studies and current news stories to make abstract ideas tangible. Encourage learners to reflect on how biology intersects with health, environmental stewardship, and community issues.
