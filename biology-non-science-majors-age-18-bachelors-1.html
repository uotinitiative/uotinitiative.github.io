Biology for Non-Science Majors (Age 18, US Bachelor’s 1)

About This Textbook

This textbook is designed for the single-semester introduction to biology course for non-science majors. It is grounded on an evolutionary basis and includes exciting features that highlight careers in the biological sciences and everyday applications of the concepts at hand.

Textbook Details
