About This Textbook

Algebra 2 deepens understanding of algebraic structures through explorations of polynomial, rational, and radical expressions, quadratic and exponential models, and sequences and series. The text balances procedural mastery with conceptual insight and plentiful applications.

Teaching & Learning Tips

Leverage technology to explore complex function families and encourage students to interpret solutions in context. Spiral review of Algebra 1 concepts is woven throughout to support retention and readiness for precalculus.
