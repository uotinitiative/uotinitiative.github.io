Workplace Software and Skills (Age 18, US Bachelor’s 1)

About This Textbook

This textbook covers applications from the Microsoft 365 (formerly Microsoft Office) suite and Google Workspace. Coverage of both suites aligns with contemporary business use and prepares students for workforce needs, especially introductory students who have had limited exposure to these software programs.
