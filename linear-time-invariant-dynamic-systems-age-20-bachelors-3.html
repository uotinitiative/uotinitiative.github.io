About This Textbook

The text develops mathematical foundations for linear system modeling, frequency response, and control, linking mechanical and electrical examples with convolution, Laplace transforms, and stability criteria.

  • Time Response via Convolution
  • Second-Order System Dynamics
  • Mechanical Systems with Multiple Degrees of Freedom
  • Laplace Block Diagrams
  • Feedback Control Concepts
  • PID Control and Stability Criteria

Teaching & Learning Tips

Integrate MATLAB/Simulink exercises and laboratory experiments so students can visualize system behavior and controller performance.
