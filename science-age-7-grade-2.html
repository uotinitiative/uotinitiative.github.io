About This Textbook

Young scientists compare how water, wind, and earth processes reshape the land. Each chapter invites students to observe real-world examples, share claims with evidence, and build simple models.

Teaching & Learning Tips

Try stream-table or sandbox investigations so students can see erosion in action. Follow each reading with a quick “explain with evidence” drawing to reinforce scientific communication.
