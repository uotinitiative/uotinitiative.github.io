About This Textbook

This streamlined text introduces the core doctrines of business law, from legal reasoning and contracts to regulation and international considerations. It equips first-year business students with the vocabulary and frameworks they need to navigate legal issues and sets the stage for deeper case-based exploration.

Teaching & Learning Tips

Pair chapter readings with contemporary case briefs or compliance headlines so learners can connect foundational rules to evolving business contexts. Encourage group debates around ethical dilemmas to reinforce the role of judgment alongside statutory interpretation.
