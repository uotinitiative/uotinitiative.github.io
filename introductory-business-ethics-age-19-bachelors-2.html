About This Textbook

This course reader frames ethics as an applied discipline. Learners analyze historical traditions, stakeholder impacts, and contemporary workplace challenges through cases, role plays, and reflective prompts.

Teaching & Learning Tips

Begin each module with a current headline and let teams map stakeholders and competing duties. Encourage students to maintain reflective journals that track how their ethical reasoning evolves during the term.
