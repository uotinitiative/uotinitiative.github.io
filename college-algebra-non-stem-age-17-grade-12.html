About This Textbook

The text pairs comprehensive algebraic content with refresher skills modules to meet the needs of learners headed for general education math pathways. Real-world scenarios and practice sets help students strengthen number sense, function analysis, and quantitative reasoning.

Teaching & Learning Tips

Connect algebraic ideas to everyday decision-making—such as budgeting, growth and decay, or data interpretation—to improve engagement. Encourage students to revisit skills modules whenever they need additional support.
