About This Textbook

Students collect data, compare models, and connect science ideas across physical, Earth, and life science strands. Graphic organizers, vocabulary supports, and practice questions make the content manageable and engaging.

Teaching & Learning Tips

Integrate simple data tables so students can track force experiments or weather observations over time. Encourage compare-and-contrast writing between plant and animal adaptations to build cross-disciplinary literacy skills.
