Math (Age 11, US Grade 6)

About This Textbook

This textbook is designed to meet the full scope-and-sequence expectations of a year-long middle-school math course that bridges elementary arithmetic and formal pre-algebra. It develops number sense, proportional reasoning, geometry, measurement, statistics, and introductory algebra in a coherent progression that prepares students for the abstract thinking demanded in later grades.
