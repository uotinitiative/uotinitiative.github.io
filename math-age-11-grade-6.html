About This Textbook

This textbook is designed to meet the full scope-and-sequence expectations of a year-long middle-school math course that bridges elementary arithmetic and formal pre-algebra. Students develop fluency with decimals and fractions, deepen their understanding of ratios and proportional reasoning, and explore key geometry and statistics concepts that prepare them for advanced study.

How to Use This Book

Educators can follow the sequence chapter by chapter for a full academic year or integrate individual units to supplement an existing curriculum. Each chapter introduces key concepts through clear explanations, examples, and practice opportunities that encourage conceptual understanding alongside procedural fluency.
