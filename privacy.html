Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how the Universal Open Textbook Initiative collects and uses information from visitors to our website.

We do not collect any personal information from visitors to our site. We do not require account creation or registration to access our textbooks. We use Google Analytics to collect anonymous information about how visitors use our website. This helps us improve our services. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect data such as the pages you visit on our site, how long you spend on each page, how you got to our site, and what kind of device you’re using. This information is processed in a way that does not identify anyone. We do not make, and do not allow Google to make, any attempt to find out the identities of those visiting our website. You can choose to disable cookies in your internet browser to prevent the collection of your information by Google Analytics when you visit our site.

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will post any changes on this page.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
