About This Textbook

This prealgebra text introduces the language and structure of algebra while reinforcing the whole number, fraction, and decimal skills students have developed in earlier grades. Each chapter builds conceptual understanding through carefully sequenced examples, multiple representations, and targeted practice that supports a wide range of learning styles.

Teaching & Learning Tips

Blend procedural fluency with concept-focused discussions. Encourage students to verbalize algebraic thinking, compare strategies, and use visual models—such as number lines and algebra tiles—to make abstract concepts concrete.
