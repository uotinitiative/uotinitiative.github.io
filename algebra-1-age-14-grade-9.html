About This Textbook

The textbook meets the scope and sequence of a first-year algebra course. Students learn to manipulate linear expressions, analyze functions, and explore polynomial and quadratic relationships through a blend of worked examples, guided practice, and real-world problem solving.

Teaching & Learning Tips

Have students interpret representations in tables, graphs, and equations together to strengthen connections. Encourage use of graphing technology or dynamic tools to explore transformations and deepen understanding of function behavior.
