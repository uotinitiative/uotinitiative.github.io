Updates

Latest Platform Updates

  • September 2025: Created dedicated pages for each available textbook, offering detailed information, metadata, and easier navigation.
  • August 2025: Added six science textbooks for Kindergarten through Grade 5.
  • July 2025: Added six textbooks in Nursing and 12 textbooks in Business.
  • June 2025: Added Chinese (Simplified) translations of Calculus Vol 1 (U.S. Bachelor’s Year 1) and Abstract Algebra (U.S. Bachelor’s Year 3).
  • May 2025: Added Math textbooks for Grade 6 and Grade 7 in English. Added Arabic translations of Calculus Vol 1 (U.S. Bachelor’s Year 1), Macroeconomics (U.S. Bachelor’s Year 1), and Abstract Algebra (U.S. Bachelor’s Year 3).
  • April 2025: Added Arabic and Chinese (Simplified) translations of Biology for Science Majors (U.S. Bachelor’s Year 1).
  • March 2025: Added a new Search page and enhanced the site-wide user interface, including improvements to design, navigation, and responsiveness.

  • More download formats.
  • A contributor portal where users can create an account, select books, and participate in improving translation quality.
