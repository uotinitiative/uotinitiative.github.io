Introduce finance majors and minors to the core concepts behind valuation, investment, and risk.

About This Textbook

The text combines conceptual explanations with calculation practice to prepare students for subsequent finance courses. Real company examples and spreadsheet-ready models show how financial managers evaluate opportunities and risk.

Teaching & Learning Tips

Integrate short spreadsheet labs after each time value of money chapter so learners manipulate cash-flow timelines. Discuss corporate governance news to show how finance decisions intersect with regulation and ethics.
