About This Textbook

This statistics textbook delivers core concepts through narrative, visuals, and worked problems that emphasize statistical reasoning. Learners explore descriptive statistics, probability, sampling, confidence intervals, hypothesis testing, and regression analysis with practical datasets.

Teaching & Learning Tips

Use technology for data visualization and computation while encouraging students to explain interpretations in plain language. Group investigations and projects strengthen connections between statistical methods and real-world questions.
