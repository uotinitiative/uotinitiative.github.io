About This Textbook

Designed for introductory anthropology courses, the text balances foundational theory with vivid case studies from around the world. It highlights how anthropologists investigate culture, language, biological evolution, and material remains to better understand human experience.

Teaching & Learning Tips

Encourage comparative discussions that connect anthropological concepts to students’ lived experiences. Integrate multimedia ethnographies, archaeological case studies, and current events to show the discipline’s relevance.
